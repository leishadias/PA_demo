Know Us

Your vision | Our motivation | Event solutions

Welcome to Pooja Arrangers, your one-stop solution for all your event and logistics needs in the picturesque city of Mangalore. With a rich legacy of providing top-notch services, we pride ourselves on being the premier choice for those seeking perfection in event management and logistical support.

At Pooja Arrangers, we specialize in a wide range of services to make your events and functions truly memorable. Whether you're planning a grand wedding, a corporate event, or a social gathering, our team is dedicated to handling every aspect of your event with finesse and precision.

We offer a comprehensive suite of services, including event management, catering, tours and travels, 24x7 ambulance service, water supply, stage setting, DJ service, and state-of-the-art sound and lighting solutions. Our commitment to excellence ensures that your event or function is executed flawlessly, leaving you free to enjoy the occasion without worry.

When you choose Pooja Arrangers, you're choosing a team of experts who are passionate about turning your dreams into reality. We look forward to being a part of your next event and exceeding your expectations. Welcome to Pooja Arrangers, where we transform your moments into unforgettable memories.
